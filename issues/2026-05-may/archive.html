Five Cities, Five Stories

The inaugural year of UPAD Times takes readers on a journey through five of Uttar Pradesh’s most historically and culturally significant cities. Each issue explores how these ancient centers of civilization are navigating the challenges and opportunities of the 21st century — preserving their sacred heritage while building infrastructure for millions of pilgrims, residents, and visitors.
